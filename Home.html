Water Heroes

Aabid Surti

Founder of Drop Dead Foundation

Drop Dead Foundation fixes leaking taps in residential houses in Mumbai, free of charge.


He read a newspaper article in 2007 that explained that a tap that dripped once every second would lead to the waste of over 1000 litres of water a month. When he became aware of the severity of the looming global water crisis, he was incentivised to found Drop Dead, which uses the tagline, “save every drop, or drop dead.”

Sandra Postel

2021 Stockholm Water Prize Laureate.

Founding director of the Global Water Policy Project and a 1980 Master of Environmental Management (MEM) alumna of Duke University.


The Stockholm Prize is among the highest honors awarded in the fields of water science and policy. Presented by the Stockholm International Water Institute and the Royal Swedish Academy of Sciences, it is sometimes referred to as the Nobel Prize of water.
